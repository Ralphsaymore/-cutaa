About the book

About Pacing for Growth

Pacing For Growth: Why Intelligent Restraint Drives Long-term Success, foreword by Marshall Goldsmith, is a fresh approach to business growth that helps leaders perform today, transform for tomorrow while managing energy. Dr. Alison Eyring; growth expert, organizational psychologist, endurance athlete and CEO of Organisation Solutions combines her 25+ years of experience advising Fortune/FTSE 500 and some of the most innovative high-growth companies on earth with what she has learnt from training for ultra-marathons. She applies endurance training concepts like finding the right pace, pushing yourself to your maximum capacity, building capabilities for the future, and conserving energy to lead business growth.

Intelligent Restraint helps organisations to avoid the wasted effort and pain of boom-splat cycles of growth, conserve and build collective energy for long-term performance, learn how to perform while preparing for the future at a winning pace and build important growth capabilities faster and cheaper. About Alison Eyring

Dr. Alison Eyring is a global thought leader on building organizational capacity for growth. Founder and Chief Executive Officer, Organisation Solutions, Alison, a trained organizational psychologist, has 25 years of experience in large-scale organization design and change and executive development. She works closely with global and regional executives from Fortune/FTSE 500 and some of the world’s most innovative high-growth companies on leadership and growth. She is also Adjunct Associate Professor at the National University of Singapore Business School.
